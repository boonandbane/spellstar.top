Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your SpellStar account and all associated data at any time. Please read the information below before submitting your request.

⚠️ What gets deleted

Deleting your account will permanently remove the following data from our servers:

  • Your email address
  • Your user ID
  • Your premium status and purchase history

This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting deletion.

💾 Data stored on your device

Spelling lists and practice history are stored locally on your device and are not affected by account deletion. To remove this data, uninstall the SpellStar app from your device.

📬 How to request deletion

Send us an email from the address associated with your SpellStar account with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will delete your account data within 30 days.

  1. Tap the button below to open your email app
  2. Send the email from the Google account you used to sign in to SpellStar
  3. We will confirm deletion within 30 days
